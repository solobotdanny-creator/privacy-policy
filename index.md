# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Treadily collects, uses, and protects your information when using the app.

## Information We Collect

We may collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Workout and fitness data
- App usage information
- Subscription and purchase information
- Crash and diagnostics data

## How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Provide personalized workout plans
- Sync workouts and progress
- Improve app performance
- Process subscriptions
- Provide customer support
- Monitor app stability and analytics

## Health & Fitness Data

Health and fitness information may be used to personalize workouts, track progress, and integrate with Apple Health or Apple Watch features when enabled by the user.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as:
- Supabase
- RevenueCat
- Apple HealthKit
- Analytics and crash reporting tools

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information and maintain data security.
